Red Sea shipping routes disrupted
What is happening

Commercial shipping through the Red Sea has declined following repeated attacks and security incidents involving cargo vessels.

Why this matters

The corridor handles roughly 12% of global maritime trade. Rerouting increases costs, delays goods, and introduces systemic uncertainty across supply chains.
