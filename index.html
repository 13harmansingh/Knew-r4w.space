Red Sea shipping routes disrupted

What is happening

Commercial shipping through the Red Sea has declined sharply following repeated security incidents involving cargo vessels.

Why this matters

The Red Sea accounts for a significant portion of global maritime trade. Rerouting increases transit time, fuel cost, and downstream pricing.
