Red Sea · Global trade Developing
Red Sea shipping routes disrupted
What is happening

Multiple international shipping companies have suspended or reduced Red Sea transit following repeated attacks on commercial vessels near the Bab el-Mandeb strait.

Why this matters

The Red Sea carries roughly 12% of global maritime trade. Rerouting ships around southern Africa adds cost, delays, and insurance risk across global supply chains.

EU AI Act enters enforcement phase
What is happening

European Union member states have begun enforcing transparency, risk classification, and compliance requirements under the AI Act.

Why this matters

The law establishes the first comprehensive regulatory framework for AI and is expected to influence global technology governance.
