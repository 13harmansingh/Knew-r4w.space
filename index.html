KNEW — The public Knowledge Network

An interactive, location-driven news platform built to show not just headlines, but context: neutral summaries, bias detection, and ownership transparency — in five languages.

  • AI summaries & bias detection — neutral 80-word summaries and LEFT/CENTER/RIGHT bias tags.
  • Interactive global map — discover stories by location and drop witness pins.
  • Multilingual — English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German with language-specific caching.
  • Realtime processing — background job queue (BullMQ + Redis) and WebSocket updates.

KNEW is for citizens, journalists, and analysts who need a clearer map of truth.

KNEW v2 — The Verification Layer for Human Reality

Enterprise trust infrastructure for capturing, verifying, and structuring real-world human data with geo-temporal context.

  • Verification Engine — secure media ingestion, GPS + timestamps, provenance hashing (SHA-256), deepfake detection.
  • Context Orchestrator — sentiment, credibility scoring, event correlation, and political inference (roadmap).
  • Intelligence Layer — enterprise REST API, verified data feeds, dashboards, and CSV/JSON exports.
  • Secure & Scalable — PostgreSQL (Neon), Supabase storage + IPFS, Clerk auth, Upstash Redis/QStash.

KNEW v2 is the backend of truth for companies, platforms, and investigators who require verified proof-of-reality.
